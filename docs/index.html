Our Answer

The SaafLoop model —
community owns the solution

Instead of waiting for government will, we give communities the tools to manage their own waste — and profit from it. Inspired by the Indore model, built for every city.

01 / Collect
🏘️

Housing Societies

RWAs connect with verified local composters. Rated pickups. Auditable records. Compliance confidence. No middlemen, no guesswork.

02 / Convert
🌱

Local Composters

Individuals and micro-units turn organic waste into quality compost products. They earn from pickups and from selling manure and derivatives on the marketplace.

03 / Connect
🚜

Farmers & Buyers

Farmers buy verified organic inputs directly from composters at below-market rates — cutting costs, improving soil health and completing the loop.

The Process

How SaafLoop works

1

Society Signs Up

Housing society commits to segregating organic waste and registers in minutes.

2

Matched with Composter

We match them with a verified local composter based on location and capacity.

3

Verified Pickups

Organic waste is collected, processed and tracked with ratings on both sides.

4

Products Sold

Compost and derivatives are listed on the marketplace. Farmers buy direct. Revenue returns to community.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is SaafLoop a government or municipal initiative?

No. SaafLoop is an independent platform designed to complement municipal systems with better coordination and transparent community-level operations — not replace or depend on them.

Will you pick up waste daily from Day 1?

Pilot rollouts are location and provider dependent. We prioritise reliability over overpromising and scale frequency where capacity is proven.

How are composters and providers verified?

We begin with identity and capacity checks, then build trust through service history, ratings and documented performance over time.

Is my data safe?

We collect only required operational data. Reports for external stakeholders are aggregated and anonymised where applicable.

What if my society and composter go direct after meeting on SaafLoop?

SaafLoop is built around long-term workflow value — compliance records, reputation, discovery and reporting continue to create operational value for both sides even as direct relationships grow.

Can I volunteer or collaborate?

Yes. Individuals, NGOs, campus groups and domain experts are welcome to collaborate on outreach, training, data and operations work.
