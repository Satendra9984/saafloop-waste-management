## Form B — Composter / Provider Onboarding

**Form title**: SaafLoop — Composter / Provider Interest Form  
**Form description**:

> If you compost, collect recyclables, or want to run a local waste management service, share your details below. SaafLoop will use this to understand your capacity and match you with nearby communities as we launch.

### Section 1 — About You / Your Organisation

1. **Full Name** (Short answer)
  *Required*
2. **Organisation / Initiative name (if any)** (Short answer)
  *Optional*
3. **Email address** (Short answer)
  *Required*
4. **Mobile number (WhatsApp preferred)** (Short answer)
  *Required*
5. **You are applying as…** (Multiple choice)
  *Required*
  - Individual composter / provider
  - Registered company
  - NGO / community group
  - Farmer / farmer group
  - Other

---

### Section 2 — Location & Service Area

1. **City** (Short answer)
  *Required*
2. **Base location / neighbourhood** (Paragraph)
  *Helper text*: “Where do you operate from today?”
3. **Distance you can realistically serve from your base (one way)** (Multiple choice)
  - Up to 3 km
  - 3–7 km
  - 7–15 km
  - City-wide (case by case)

---

### Section 3 — Services & Capacity

1. **What services do you currently offer or want to offer?** (Checkboxes)
  *Required*
  - Door-to-door collection of segregated waste
  - Organic waste composting (on-site for societies)
  - Organic waste composting (off-site at my facility)
  - Dry waste / recyclables aggregation
  - E-waste collection (small scale)
  - Advisory / training / workshops
  - Other (short answer)
2. **Approximate daily capacity you can handle** (Multiple choice)
  *Helper text*: Choose the closest range for organic waste you can process or collect per day.
  - Up to 50 kg / day
  - 50–200 kg / day
  - 200–500 kg / day
  - 500+ kg / day
  - Not sure yet
3. **Do you already produce compost or other products?** (Checkboxes)
  - Bagged compost / vermicompost
    - Bulk compost (loose)
    - Biogas / slurry
    - Recycled material products (paper, plastic, etc.)
    - Not yet, but planning to
4. **How do you prefer to charge for your services?** (Checkboxes)
  *Required*
  - Monthly subscription per society / client
  - Per pickup / per visit
  - Per kg of waste handled
  - Revenue share on compost / product sales
  - Open to suggestions based on model

---

### Section 4 — Infrastructure & Readiness

1. **What infrastructure do you currently have?** (Checkboxes)
  - Land / space for composting
    - Existing compost pits / units
    - Vehicle (two-wheeler with carrier / three-wheeler / small truck)
    - Storage space for recyclables
    - Basic safety gear for workers
    - None yet, looking to start
2. **Do you have any existing partnerships with societies or institutions?** (Paragraph)
  *Optional*
3. **On a scale of 1–5, how ready are you to start or expand services in the next 3–6 months?** (Linear scale 1–5)
  *Labels*: 1 = Just exploring, 5 = Ready to start now

---

### Section 5 — Matching & Consent

1. **What kind of clients are you most interested in?** (Checkboxes)
  - Housing societies / RWAs
    - Small businesses and offices
    - Schools / colleges
    - Restaurants / food businesses
    - Farmer groups (for product sales)
2. **Shortly describe how you currently handle or plan to handle waste responsibly.** (Paragraph)
  *Helper text*: This helps us understand your approach and alignment with SaafLoop’s principles.
3. **Consent** (Checkbox)
  *Required*  
  - I agree that SaafLoop can contact me about pilots, leads and opportunities, and may use aggregated data (never personal phone/email) to improve the platform.

**Confirmation message**:

> Thank you for your interest in joining SaafLoop as a composter / provider.  
>
> As we start pilots in your city, we’ll get in touch to understand your capacity in more detail and explore how we can connect you to nearby societies and buyers.
