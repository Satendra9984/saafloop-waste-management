Our Answer

The SaafLoop model —
community owns the solution

Instead of waiting for government will, we give communities the tools to manage their own waste — and profit from it. Inspired by the Indore model, built for every city.

01 / Collect
🏘️

Housing Societies

RWAs connect directly with verified local composters. No middlemen. Rated pickups. Real accountability. Weekly organic waste collection that actually happens.

02 / Convert
🌱

Local Composters

Individuals and micro-units turn organic waste into quality compost. They earn income from pickups and from selling the finished manure on our marketplace.

03 / Connect
🚜

Farmers Get Manure

Farmers buy verified organic manure directly from composters at below-market rates — cutting fertilizer costs while getting better soil inputs. Everyone wins.

The Process

How SaafLoop works

1

Society Signs Up

Housing society commits to separating organic waste. Takes 5 minutes to register.

2

Matched with Composter

We match them with a verified local composter based on location and capacity.

3

Weekly Pickups

Organic waste is collected, composted, and tracked. Both sides rate each other.

4

Manure Sold to Farmers

Compost is listed on the marketplace. Farmers buy direct. Revenue returns to community.
